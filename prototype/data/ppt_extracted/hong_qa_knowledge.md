## PDF Specification Q&A

**Q: What is bending location tolerance?**
A: 0.300 mm

**Q: What is FPC design guideline of De-cap ?**
A: When a flex needs to have different numbers of copper layers for different regions of the same flex, de-cap isrequired at the layer transition to cut away the excess layers. For example, a flex may have two layers of copper forhalf and be only one layer for the other half, with the layer transition being the area for de-cap. The cut awayprocess is done with either a pre-punch of PI and bonding sheet (usually only for 3+ layer flexes) before laminationor with a laser after lamination

**Q: What is the bonding adhesive patch back?**
A: After drill hole plasma and wet desmear, the adhesive between 2 FCCL will has etch back. This is to ensure the palm and wet desmear are effective to remove adhesive.

**Q: What is the LPI or Solder mask thickness**
A: 4-39um

**Q: What is dimension of EMI shield to coverlay edge ?**
A: 0.400 mm
